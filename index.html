Tongkyu Kim

Researcher, International Cooperation Division, Korea Development Institute (KDI GKEDC)

Interdisciplinary researcher with expertise in climate governance and environmental behavior. Research focuses on the integrity and implementation of Article 6 mechanisms, decision-making in carbon markets, and behavioral responses to transboundary mitigation policies. Demonstrated record of high-impact research and policy analysis across academic, governmental, and multilateral settings.

Previously, he served at the Climate and Energy Cooperation Center of the Korean Ministry of Foreign Affairs, where he supported climate diplomacy and presented Korea's climate missions to international policymakers. A notable moment was delivering Korea's Carbon Free Energy Initiative to ambassadors and climate leaders at the Vatican. He also contributed to government and corporate sustainable development projects, including KSP policy consultation reports and the Energy Mega City Act.

He holds an M.A. in Environmental Policy and a B.A. in International Studies from Hanyang University, and his multidisciplinary research continues to advance the global shift toward cleaner energy.
